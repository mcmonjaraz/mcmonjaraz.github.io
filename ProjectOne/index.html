Pinky and Index

For the first time ever the famous finger performers Pinky and Index have come together on the big screen to create an unparalleld performance. Both performers wich together add up to over 50 days of working experience in the contemporany figer art performcance have brought there distinc styles and merged them in a unique style they call "Molto Expresso Dedo". The russian conservatory style of Pinky(Acting as Billie) contrasts in a pacific way with the the liberal Argentinian education od Index(performing as Micheal).

Manesha and Sohyun

An extremly talented co-production that overcame both time and budget restrains, and out of nothing craftyed this beautifull piece. Manesha comes from a background of brodway theater, having worked in major performances like Jesus Superstar and Cats in major venues all over the world. On the other hand Sohyun has worked in the experimental film making for over two weeks, and brings her unique one static shot techniques to the filming of this short film/performance.

Mateo

A long time film scorer graduated from CLU(CommLab University), Mateo brought his ilegal music downloading techniques combined with his scarce knowledge of Garage band to bring the music to live in this work. THe Challenges of not having an Iphone cable and airdrop not working were elements that defenetly shapes his work both in production and post production.

Amber

The production in this project was specially challenging and only a producer like Amber could have brought all these diffrent talents into unison. Her leadership is printed on this beautiful short film. Amber, who recived her production education in IMC(IMovie Colleges) left her mark in the fil with new transitions that go way beyond inovation.

Story

A story exploring the true meaning of Micheals Jackson's Billie Jean, performed by unique actors. This finger performance of BIllie Jean was actually Micheal Jacksons last will, after years of researching for the best suited performers, finally the right fingrs where found. One of the most challenging films produced in the last decade which breaks through the possibilities of film.
